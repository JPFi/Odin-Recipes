Now that we've gotten the boring items out of the whey (pun intended), let's go over the the important ingredients to create this recipe.

For a list, you'll need the following items:

  • Egg Whites-$4.99 (this is mostly for controlled weight loss and muscle gain, otherwise you can use whole eggs, preferably brown for more calories)
  • Baby Spinach ack-$2.59
  • Turkey Bacon-$3.99
  • Onions-$3.99
  • Tomatoes-$1.49
  • Black Pepper-$1.39
  • Fat-free Mozzarella Cheese

Obviously, for an omelette, you can create many combinations but I like to keep it simple. You can add other sources of protein as well, so long as they're low fat. I'm an advocate for turkey bacon, obviously.

Step By Step Process

  • On a frying pan, add some fat-free avocado or vegetable oil and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes on medium heat.
    • I personally like to buy the spray oil as it heats up the pan quicker.
  • Once the pan is hot, add your: baby spinach, turkey bacon (or protein of choice), sliced tomatoes and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes. You want to ensure the ingredients are cooked evenly, so spread them out on the pan.
  • Then, on a bowl, mix your egg whites (white, or brown egg) with your black pepper, and add it to your pan accordingly. Allow some time for the eggs to mix in well with the ingredients.
  • After a few minutes, flip back and forth to evenly cook the omelette.
  • Once cooked, add mozzarella cheese and cook on low-heat for 5 minutes. This will allow the cheese to dissolve for a delicious taste.
